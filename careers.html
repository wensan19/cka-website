Interior Designer

Bring concept, detail and coordination together across refined architecture and interior projects.

01

Job Responsibilities

  • Conceptualize concept and schematic designs
  • Carry out feasibility studies
  • Prepare interior design presentation drawings and specifications
  • Prepare interior design tender drawings
  • Assist in tendering processes
  • Attend clients'/consultants' meetings and site meetings
02

Qualifications

  • Degree/Diploma/Higher Nitec/Nitec in Architecture or Interior Design or Architectural Technology equivalent
  • Minimum 3 years' relevant experience
  • Must be proficient in AutoCAD, SketchUp, Enscape and Photoshop
03

Personal Qualities

  • Possess creative design thinking and excellent design detailing skills
  • Good team player and interpersonal skills
  • Proactive and meticulous

Preferred Software

AutoCAD SketchUp Enscape Photoshop
