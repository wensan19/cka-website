About Us

The Company

CKA Design Pte Ltd provides a full range of services that include Feasibility Studies, Building and Interior Designs, Contract Documentation and Project Management. CKA Design Pte Ltd provides client-oriented professional services, with an experienced design team and project consultants. The firm believes that the good design of a place shall fit its purpose and meet the end user's requirements, together with a high level of quality and professional standards both in design and project management. The aim is to ensure a high level of quality work and project completion within the budget and time frame. The firm's key projects are Shilla Cosmetics & Perfumes Duty Free Shops at Changi Airport Terminals 1, 2, 3 and 4, Chanel Beauty Lounge, Loyang Tua Pek Kong Temple, Kong Meng San Phor Kark See Monastery, among others. Some other ongoing projects include Palelai Buddhist Temple, One Shenton, and Singapore Recreation Club.
