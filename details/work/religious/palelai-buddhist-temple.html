Religious

Palelai Buddhist Temple @ Bedok Walk

A phased temple renewal shaped around worship, meditation, accessibility and the growth of the Palelai Buddhist Temple community.

Temple Renewal

A new main shrine for a growing community

To cater to the ever-growing community of Palelai Buddhist Temple, a new prominent 2 Storey Main Shrine building with Basement will be built with an increase in Praying & Meditation area.

The Main Shrine & Chedi buildings will also be equipped with new Accessible lifts to cater for the elderly & disabled devotees, allowing easier access to various facilities.

Milestone 1.0

Existing Temple Condition

The redevelopment begins with a careful reading of the existing temple, its devotional routines and the spatial needs of a larger community before the new works are introduced.

Milestone 2.0

Factory Visit and Craft Review

Material and component reviews help align the construction process with the temple's architectural intent, ensuring key elements are checked before installation on site.

Milestone 3.0

Ground Breaking Ceremony

The ceremony marks the transition from planning into construction, bringing together the temple community, project team and stakeholders at an important moment in the redevelopment journey.

Artist Impressions

Main Shrine Building

The new shrine building is envisioned as a composed religious landmark, increasing prayer and meditation areas while improving access through new lift connections for elderly and disabled devotees.
